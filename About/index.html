About Tenebris

Tenebris tattoo parlor was founded 10 years ago by
Cash Mat - a self-taught tattoo enthusiast from the
great city of Chicago. Tenebris has made its mark as
Chicago's renowned tattoo destination. Our main goal
is to turn your inspiration into a fine work of art and
craft the best possible tattoos. Tenebris has become a
hub of tattoo artists and aficionados from all walks of
life, disciplines and ages.

Our studio is a hub for passionate artists and tattoo
connoisseurs, who provide the best possible
experience, taking all customer requests into
consideration. At Tenebris, we ensure a safe
environment and pride ourselves with the highest
industry standards.

- Cash Mat
